Privacy Policy

Food Truck Rewards ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Food Truck Rewards mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, and password when you create an account (vendor or customer)
  • Vendor Information: Business/truck name, owner name, phone number, operating states, and brand customization preferences
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers — all payment data is handled by Stripe, Inc. under their own privacy policy
  • Profile Photos: Profile pictures you voluntarily upload
  • Location Information: Vendor-provided daily business location and weekly schedule (street address or general area — not precise GPS coordinates)
  • User-Generated Content: Social feed posts, reviews, ratings, and comments you create
  • Communications: Messages or emails you send to our support team

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, check-ins, points earned and redeemed, spin wheel results, and visit history
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser type for compatibility purposes
  • Push Notification Tokens: Expo push tokens to deliver notifications you have opted into (optional)
  • Referral Data: Referral codes used during signup to award loyalty bonuses

C. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect precise real-time GPS location from your device
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not collect data from your contacts, calendar, or other apps
  • We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising networks

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process vendor subscription payments through Stripe
  • Operate the loyalty rewards program (award points, track visits, process redemptions)
  • Display vendor locations and schedules to customers
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (loyalty updates, vendor announcements)
  • Show your profile picture and name within the app
  • Enable vendor-to-customer social feed posts and customer reviews
  • Generate leaderboards showing top loyalty members at each truck
  • Send transactional emails (password reset, account confirmation)
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and maintain the App
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

A. Within the App

  • Vendor to Customer: Vendors' truck names, profile pictures, locations, hours, and social posts are visible to customers who join their loyalty program
  • Customer to Vendor: Customer names, profile pictures, point totals, and visit counts are visible to vendors whose program they have joined. Customer reviews and ratings are visible to vendors
  • Leaderboards: Customer names and point totals may appear on public leaderboards within a truck's loyalty program

B. Service Providers

  • Stripe, Inc.: Payment processing for vendor subscriptions
  • Supabase: Secure database hosting and storage
  • Expo / Expo Push Notifications: Delivery of opted-in push notifications
  • Render / Railway: Application hosting and infrastructure
  • Google (Gmail SMTP): Transactional email delivery

All service providers are contractually required to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

C. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of Food Truck Rewards, its users, or the public.

D. Business Transfer

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice in the App.

4. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Upon account deletion, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Vendor subscriptions: Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Password reset tokens: Automatically expire within 1 hour of generation

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with expiration
  • Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You may access and update your personal information at any time through your account profile settings within the App.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through the Profile section of the App or by contacting us at support@foodtruckrewards.app. Deletion removes your personal data from our active systems within 30 days.

Push Notifications

You may opt out of push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. This does not affect transactional emails.

Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at support@foodtruckrewards.app.

Do Not Track

Our App does not respond to Do Not Track signals as we do not track users across third-party websites.

7. Children's Privacy

Food Truck Rewards is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted information to us, please contact us at support@foodtruckrewards.app.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party services (such as Google Maps for location directions). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. International Users

Food Truck Rewards is operated in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@foodtruckrewards.app.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page and notify active users via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
